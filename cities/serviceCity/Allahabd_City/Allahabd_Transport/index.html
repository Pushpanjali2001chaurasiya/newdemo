About Us

Prayagraj City Transport Service Ltd. is a city based service under JnNURM scheme of Government of India which operates primarily in the city of Prayagraj. It covers the entire city and is used by daily commuters. Prayagraj City Transport Service Ltd. has 10 Non A/c low Floor Marcopolo Buses, 58 big ordinary buses with air suspension And 59 Mini Buses, totaling 127. These types of buses have been very successful in Prayagraj is liked by all sections of commuters it is liked very much for environment friendly, easy, comfortable & safe transportation by daily commuters like office goers, students, senior citizen and ladies.
Directors of Prayagraj City Transport Services Limited are Sanjay Prasad, Surya Prakash Mishra, Amarendra Kumar Jha, Ashok Kumar, Anil Kumar Misra, Anil Kumar Singh, Chandra Prakash, Ajay Kumar Upadhyay, Ashish Goel, Sagir Ahmed Ansari, Bhanu Chandra Goswami, Harish Chandra and Ujjwal Kumar.
